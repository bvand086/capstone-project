Benjamin van der Woerd

MD MSc FRCS(C)

Summary

Dr. Benjamin van der Woerd has recently joined the McMaster University Department of Surgery in the Division of Otolaryngology-Head and Neck Surgery.

He received his undergraduate degree in Health Sciences at the University of Ottawa before attending medical school at McMaster University DeGroote School of Medicine. He completed his residency training at Western University’s Schulich School of Medicine in Otolaryngology-Head and Neck Surgery. During his training, he completed a Master of Science in Surgery at Western University in 2019. In July 2022, he completed his sub-specialty training at the University of Southern California Keck School of Medicine in Los Angeles, California in Laryngology and Care of the Professional Voice.

He specializes in laryngeal, voice, airway, and swallowing disorders. His clinical interests include voice disorders in professional and non-professional voice users, swallowing disorders, endoscopic and open management of upper airway disorders, and in-office laryngeal procedures.
